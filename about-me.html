More About Me

A personal blog

Above everything, I am always working towards my end goal of becoming an 'Inventor', whether it is in Engineering, Computing, DIY at home or even my hobbies. The choices I make generally revolve around this idea I have on my mind. I can't remember exactly when I first started wanting this but I remember my childhood was watching shows such as Transformers & Gundam Wing which were mainly mechanised robots and I was always making stuff from scratch thanks to my Godmother who kept a box of scrap cardboard boxes, tissue rolls & tape where I could build anything I had on my mind from small towers to wearable gadgets.

I also have many interests and hobbies that I like to pursue, some I know might take me an age to master but if I can keep at them for as long as possible I know I can get there

My free time goes on slowly renovating my home which I've had for a couple of years now. I've worked on manual projects like plastering my fireplace or digging out my garden for a patio to adding small smart gadgets around my home like automated lights and installing various sensors which control the temperatures and safety of my home. As I work on more projects around my home I will post on my Personal page

Laser eye surgery

One of the best decisions I made was to get Laser Eye Surgery at the age of 23. I had really strong glasses that seemed to keep getting thicker and the weight of these glasses pushed me towards getting my eyes treated. I have had no problems since it seems like and somehow it even sorted out my itchy-eyes during peak Hayfever season!
