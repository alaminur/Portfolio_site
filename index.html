A personal message

One day I was told 'A jack of all trades, a master of none' after I explained my weekly routine to an experienced colleague. At first it had me thinking if I should hone my skills into one subject like so many do and I tried this for a while but it wasn't long after that I realised I didn't want to share that ideology. There was always more things to learn that I didn't know and so much time in a day that could be used

I carried on with my own mindset for years with the same comment said to me but then I came across the full saying 'A jack of all trades, a master of none but often times better than a master of one' and now I can return the favour to them
